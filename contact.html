Common Questions

Quick answers before you send a message.

How quickly will you reply to my message?
I check messages every day and aim to reply within 24–48 hours. For urgent issues like a broken tool, I usually respond much faster — often within a few hours. You'll receive a reply to the email address you provided in the form.
Can I suggest a new tool for Toolkit Pro?
Absolutely — and I encourage it! Every tool on this site came from a real need, often suggested by users like you. Use the contact form, select "Suggest a New Tool," and describe what you need. If it's something that would help many people, I'll add it to my build list.
Do you offer freelance web development services?
Yes, I'm available for freelance web and mobile application development projects. If you have a project in mind, use the contact form (select "Collaboration / Partnership") and describe what you're looking for. I'll review your request and reply with my availability and rates.
Can I write a guest blog post for Toolkit Pro?
Yes! I accept well-written, original guest posts about image optimization, web performance, SEO, blogging tools, and affiliate marketing. The content must be helpful, written in plain English, and not published elsewhere. Reach out with your topic idea first before writing the full article.
Is there a phone number I can call?
No, I only communicate via email and the contact form. This keeps things organized and ensures I can give every message the attention it deserves. Please use the form above or email me directly — I respond to all genuine messages.
