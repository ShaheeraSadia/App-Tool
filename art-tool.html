How to Use the Art Tool

A complete guide to every tool and feature in the Toolkit Pro Art Studio.

Pen & Brush

Pen gives clean thin lines. Brush creates wide, soft strokes. Marker adds semi-transparent bold lines. Adjust size and opacity for different effects.

Spray Paint

Creates a soft airbrush effect. Hold the mouse still for concentrated spray, or sweep quickly for a light mist. Great for shading and gradients.

Shapes

Draw lines, rectangles, circles, and triangles by clicking and dragging. The shape previews as you drag and is finalized when you release.

Fill Bucket

Click any area to flood-fill it with your selected color. Works like a paint bucket — fills the connected region with the same color instantly.

Eraser

Erase any part of your drawing. Adjust size to erase precisely or broadly. The eraser removes pixels and perfectly restores the white canvas.

Download PNG

Save your artwork as a high-quality PNG file. The download includes the white background — ready to share on social media, Pinterest, or print.
