FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Toolkit Pro and our free tools.

Is Toolkit Pro free to use?
Yes — all tools on Toolkit Pro are 100% free with no sign-up, no account, and no hidden fees. We support the site through Google AdSense advertising and Amazon affiliate links.
Are my images uploaded to your servers?
No — never. All image processing (compression, color extraction, design generation) happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. Your privacy is fully protected.
What image formats does the compressor support?
Toolkit Pro's Image Compressor supports JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF formats up to 10MB in size. You can also convert between formats — convert PNG to WebP for smaller files, or JPEG to PNG for transparency support.
How does the image compressor work?
Our tool uses lossy and lossless compression via the browser's Canvas API to strip unnecessary metadata and optimize pixel data. You can control quality (10–100%), resize scale, and output format to get exactly the result you need.
Can I use Toolkit Pro on mobile?
Yes! Toolkit Pro is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. All tools are optimized for mobile use — compress images, extract colors, and generate QR codes directly from your phone's browser.
Do the Amazon affiliate links cost me anything?
No — never. If you purchase through our Amazon affiliate links, you pay exactly the same price as on Amazon directly. We earn a small referral commission from Amazon which helps keep all our tools free for everyone.
